Why AZM Group

Built on 50 Years of Excellence

AZM Group is a leading supplier of high-quality ceramic and porcelain tiles, large-format slabs, sanitary ware, hardware and marble customization solutions. With strong sourcing networks in the UK, India, China and the GCC, we bring global quality to the UAE market.

Project-Centric Service

Our processes are designed for efficiency and clear communication — from material specification to final delivery. We understand your timelines and are committed to meeting them, every time.

Expert Curation

Our collections are tailored for the modern UAE market — featuring the latest global trends alongside timeless classics, all selected for durability and exceptional aesthetic quality.

Local Expertise, Global Sourcing

As your UAE-based partner, we offer the convenience of local stock availability with the breadth of international quality — sourced from UK, India, China and the GCC for your complete assurance.

In-House Fabrication

Our dedicated marble and porcelain workshop — Hutaib Marble Trading LLC — offers waterjet cutting, bridge saw precision, and fully bespoke fabrication for luxury interior applications.
