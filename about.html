About AZM Group

The Foundation of Excellence

Founded on the legacy of the Pedhiwala Group in India (1971), AZM Group expanded to the UAE in 2010. Rebranded in 2013, we have grown to become a cornerstone in the UAE's construction and interior design sectors.

We serve a broad spectrum of clients across the UAE and Oman, including leading contractors, developers, architects, and retail consumers.

Global Sourcing Network

We pride ourselves on an extensive global sourcing network, bringing the finest building materials from the UK, India, China, Europe, and the GCC directly to your projects.

👁️

Our Vision

To be the UAE's most trusted and innovative supplier of premium building materials, setting industry standards for quality, reliability, and customer excellence while shaping the future of construction and design.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive building material solutions that empower contractors, developers, architects, and designers to bring their visions to life through quality products, expert guidance, and exceptional service.

History & Evolution

Our Journey

1971

The Legacy

Pedhiwala Group established in India, starting a 50+ year expertise in global building material supply.

2010

UAE Expansion

Strategic expansion into the UAE, bringing decades of technical heritage to the booming Middle East construction market.

2013

Brand Evolution

Rebranded as AZM Group (Al Zahra Al Malakia), unifying tiles, sanitary ware, and marble into a cohesive premier brand.

TODAY

Regional Leader

A trusted partner for contractors across the UAE and Oman, delivering quality-driven multi-category solutions.
